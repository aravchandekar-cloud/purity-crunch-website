About Purity Crunch

Most store-bought banana chips are soft, stale, and frankly disappointing.

We don't just fry chips. We export an authentic piece of Bhusawal heritage.

Located right in the heart of Bhusawal — India's famous banana capital — Purity Crunch was born out of a desire to share truly authentic Indian snacks with the world.

We follow a meticulous 100% handmade process using unique, traditional spice blends you cannot find anywhere else. No shortcuts, no artificial flavors — just pure crunch.

Our 4-Step Promise

Every chip that leaves our facility follows the same disciplined, handcrafted process — with zero compromise.

1

Farm Fresh

Sourced directly from Bhusawal's finest banana plantations at peak ripeness.

2

Hand Slice

Each banana is expertly sliced by our artisans for uniform thickness and perfect crunch.

3

Spice & Fry

4

Pack & Export
