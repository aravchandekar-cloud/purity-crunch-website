# Purity Crunch Customer Review Script

## Script Brief
**Topic:** Customer Review of Purity Crunch Banana Chips
**Format:** Short-form (Reel/TikTok/Short)
**Target length:** ~60 seconds
**Tone:** Enthusiastic, genuine, and punchy
**Speaker profile:** Relatable Indian creator in his 30s with glasses, a mustache, and a casual plaid shirt.

---

## HOOK (0:00-0:04 ~4s)

[CUT TO: tight shot, speaker (Indian man with glasses, mustache, plaid shirt) holds a single golden banana chip close to the camera]

**"Listen to this."**

[SFX: Loud, incredibly crisp CRUNCH sound as he snaps the chip]

[TEXT ON SCREEN: "The Ultimate Crunch"]

---

## SEGMENT 1: THE QUALITY (0:04-0:18 ~14s)

[CUT TO: medium shot, speaker looking directly at camera, enthusiastic]

Most store-bought banana chips are soft, stale, and frankly disappointing. 

[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Soft & Stale = Disappointing"]

But these? These are Purity Crunch. 

[B-ROLL: close up of hands tossing perfectly golden classic banana chips into a rustic wooden bowl]

They're 100% handmade in Bhusawal—the Banana Capital of India. And you can literally taste the difference in every single bite.

[TEXT ON SCREEN: "100% Handmade in Bhusawal"]

---

## SEGMENT 2: AUTHENTIC FLAVOR (0:18-0:35 ~17s)

[CUT TO: tight shot, speaker holds up a pack or handful of 'Traditional Masala' chips]

[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Secret Traditional Masala"]

It’s not just the crunch. It’s the flavor. 

[B-ROLL: slow-motion dusting of rich, red traditional masala and black pepper over fresh chips]

They use a closely guarded, authentic spice blend that perfectly balances the heat and the savory kick. It tastes like it came straight from a local Indian farm market.

[SFX: subtle sizzle/cooking sound effect]

---

## SEGMENT 3: PREMIUM EXPORT (0:35-0:50 ~15s)

[CUT TO: medium shot, speaker standing in front of stacked premium export boxes]

And they stay perfectly fresh because of this premium, export-ready packaging. 

[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Export-Ready Quality"]

[B-ROLL: quick shots of sealed 200g pouches and bulk shipping boxes]

They ship globally, so whether you're down the street or across the world, they arrive tasting like they were fried this morning.

---

## RECAP + CTA (0:50-0:60 ~10s)

[CUT TO: tight shot, speaker points aggressively but enthusiastically at the camera]

**"If you want the real, authentic taste of India..."**

[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Try the Authentic Taste of India"]

Hit the link below to try Purity Crunch for yourself. I promise, you'll never go back to regular chips.

[SFX: upbeat outro music fades in]
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Link in Description / Bio"]
